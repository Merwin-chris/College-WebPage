Department of Architecture


Welcome to the Department of Architecture at Periyar Maniammai Institute of science and technology. The Department established in the academic year 1995-96 has continuously made significant strides in the field of Architectural education with all round performance academically, socially and environmentally. It is marching towards 25 years of excellence. The well designed Architectural program encapsulates all the aspects of the current profession and society. The Department has a strong tradition of fostering independent designing , research and social responsibility.


PROGRAMME OVERVIEW:

The Architectural degree is designed in such a way that it fulfills the educational component of professional certifying body Council of Architecture (CoA). Bachelor of Architecture (B.Arch) is a 5 year full-time programme comprising 10 semesters. The Program is a comprehensive amalgamation of professionally oriented course design and Outcome Based Education system. The peer learning methodologies adopted in the Department, facilitates interaction and knowledge transmission and inculcate collaborative attitude in the profession. The curriculum is designed by experts from academia and professionals to meet the various multi-disciplinary knowledge that an architect must possess to design the built environment.





Department of Engineering


The Department of Engineering was established in the year of 2009. The Department has grown significantly and offers B.Tech.- Mechanical Engineering,B.Tech - Electronics and Communication Engineering, M.Tech.-Renewable Energy and Ph.D. courses with a well qualified and experienced team of faculty specialized in different fields of Mechanical Engineering committed to provide quality education.


PROGRAMME OVERVIEW:

Mechanical Engineering is a discipline of engineering that applies the basic principles of physics and material science for analysis, design, manufacturing and maintenance of mechanical systems. The Department was established in the year of 2008 and has grown significantly. The Department has smart class room facility and CNC Machines Lab sponsored by Bosch-Rexroth India Ltd. It has also received research grants for an amount of 153 lakhs as total grants from various Govt. funding agencies such as DST and MNRE, New Delhi. The Department is constantly promoting renewable energy activities and the students actively involved in fabrication of renewable energy products. Well qualified and experienced team of faculty specialized in different fields of Mechanical Engineering are committed to provide quality education.




Department of Arts & Sciences


The Department of Education was started in 2009 with the objectives of enhancing the professional Competencies and skills of serving teachers in Elementary, Secondary and Senior Secondary Schools. The Department offers B.Ed programmes from the academic year 2009 – 2010, after duly recognized by NCTE, Bangalore. The department also offering B.Sc. B.Ed (Four Year Integrated Programme) from the academic year 2015, M.Phil. programmes (Full Time/Part Time) and Ph.D. programme (Full Time / Part Time) from the academic year 2010 – 2011. Curriculum innovation is given priority by the University to make the courses with job oriented. Besides the regular activities, the faculty members of the Education Department are actively involved in research oriented activities also along with the publication of articles in national and international journals. The Department organizes workshops, seminars and Conferences to enrich the academic community.


PROGRAMME OVERVIEW:

The Department offers 4 year B. Sc. B. Ed integrated programme.It is a unique programmes comprising 3 major science subjects vizMathematics, Physics, Chemistry / Computer Science along with B. Ed degree. It is designed to integrate content and pedagogy by using various intervention activities. Generally it requires 5 year durationi.e.(3+2) to complete B.Sc. and B.Ed. whereas this integrated B.Sc. B. Ed helps the students to complete the same B.Sc.B.Ed. in 4 years.
